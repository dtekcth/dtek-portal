What kind of emails would you like to receive?

The newsletter will get you weekly updates about activities at the division and might include promotional career related content managed by DAG.
If you give us expected year of graduation you'll recive a notification to update your preferences when to alumni updates and some content from DAG not aplicable to you may be filtered.
If you opt in to receive Alumni Updates we might occasionally send you updates we think are of interest, such as invites to anniversaries.
If we plan to change our content policy you'll receive an email notifying you of that.

You can change your subscription preferences at any time by clicking the link in the footer of our emails. For information about our privacy practices, please visit our website.

We use MailChimp as our marketing platform. By clicking below to subscribe, you acknowledge that your information will be transferred to MailChimp for processing. Learn more about MailChimp's privacy practices here.
