Welcome

This is the DTEK-portal, containing a bunch of nice links to places you might want to go.

The Student Division of Computer Science and Engineering, "Data" (officially "Datateknologsektionen" in Swedish), is a nonprofit organization for all students at the Computer Science and Engineering programme and related master's programmes at Chalmers University of Technology. We arrange receptions for newly admitted students both bachelors and masters, host parties, monitor your education and much more.


We are a part of Chalmers Student Union
